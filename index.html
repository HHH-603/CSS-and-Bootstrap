About Me

Hi! My name is Henry Hamilton Holmes, but all of my friends call me "Hank." I was born in 1990, and was born-and-raised in Concord, New Hampshire. I graduated from Randolph-Macon College in 2012 with a BA in Political Science and Music, and since then I have worked/lived in Juneau, Alaska; Washington, DC; Surry, Maine; and State College, Pennsylvania.

Before enrolling at UNC Charlotte I was an Account Executive at EveryAction, a nonprofit software company based in Washington, DC. While working there I helped equip clients with bespoke CRM solutions tailored to their specific organizational needs (fundraising, civic engagement, online advocacy, volunteer management, etc.).

When I'm not busy with studying for 30-40 hours/week for The Coding Bootcamp, I enjoy hiking big mountains, listening to classic rock, and exploring the Queen City.
